Job Description

We are hiring an MIS Executive to join our logistics team in Delhi. If you are skilled in Excel and have strong client-handling capabilities, we want to hear from you!

  • Prepare and maintain daily, weekly, and monthly MIS reports with accuracy and timeliness.
  • Conduct client visits to strengthen relationships and understand service feedback.
  • Manage and respond to customer queries and escalations via calls and emails.
  • Follow up on bookings, track shipments (FTL & PTL) from first to last mile.
  • Ensure proper documentation and coordination for accurate service delivery.
  • Collaborate with internal departments to resolve logistics or operational issues promptly.

Required Skills

  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel (VLOOKUP, Pivot Tables, Filtering, PowerPivot).
  • Good communication skills (verbal & written).
  • Understanding of logistics operations & terms (FTL/PTL).
  • Strong client handling and follow-up skills.
  • Detail-oriented and organized in handling administrative tasks.
